ABOUT

Even more places for you to not want to talk with me.

I'm Kusane Hexaku, a puzzles and games (not just video games, but games as a concept) enthusiast. I've never done work in major hunts, though I've done a few in smaller hunts.

Some of the more real, tangible past work of mine would be the Concise Trickeries Compilation Mini Hunt made for the Cracking the Cryptic Discord community, and a few small one-off hunts I've made as things like birthday gift or Christmas gift, and even then it's still not much.

I have been writing puzzles on and off since 2016, when I first discovered Puzzled Pint and fell in love with the world of puzzle hunts.
