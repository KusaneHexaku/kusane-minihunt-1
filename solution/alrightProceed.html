Solution: Alright, Proceed.

Answer: PILOT BAND


The puzzles gives nine seemingly unrelated phrases. The title of the puzzle, "Alright, Proceed.", is a hint for the band OK GO. Additonally, the flavour text is made up of three different OK GO song titles ("Here It Goes Again","Don't Ask Me", and "Do What You Want", which has two different music videos so I'm going to link them both here and here.

We can look up these nine phrases, and sure enough they are all lines from an OK GO song.

While it is specified that the redacted words are not involved in the extraction steps, the number of █s in each instances of the redaction matches the number of letters in the redacted word.

Here we notice a couple things :

- The lyrics all appears at different times into their songs, but overall relatively early.

- The given lines appears only once in each of their song.


So, we can find how many lines into the song do they appear at, then use that position to index into the song titles.
